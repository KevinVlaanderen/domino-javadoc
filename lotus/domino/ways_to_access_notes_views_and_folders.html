Accessing views and folders

The NotesView class lets you locate documents within and perform operations on views and folders. The NotesView class does not have a New method. You access views and folders through the CreateView method, GetView method, and Views property of NotesDatabase. If you have access to a view in the UI, you can use the View property of NotesUIView.
